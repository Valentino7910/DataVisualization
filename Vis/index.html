Statistics on COVID deaths in OECD countries

The bar chart visualizes the latest data on all-cause deaths, excess mortality, and COVID-19 deaths across OECD countries, organized by week. Each bar represents a specific week, with the height of the bar indicating the number of deaths recorded during that period. The chart provides insights into the temporal patterns of mortality rates, highlighting the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on overall mortality and excess deaths compared to typical trends. By examining the variations in bar heights over time and comparing across countries, viewers can gain a deeper understanding of the pandemic's toll on public health and mortality rates across different regions.
